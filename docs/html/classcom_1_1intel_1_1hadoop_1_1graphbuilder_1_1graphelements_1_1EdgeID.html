Detailed Description

The identifying information of an edge: Its source, its destination, and its label.

This class is used in the initial reducer of an

GraphGenerationJob

to detect and handle duplicate edges. Edges are considered duplicate if they have identical source, destination, and label. Property maps are not used for purposes of comparison.
